We rotate or track chores fairly. Shared spaces are cleaned on a schedule we all agree.

4. Food & supplies

We label personal food. Shared groceries are tracked and split as we decide.

5. Conflict resolution

We address issues directly first, then use a neutral mediator or landlord if needed.

6. Move-out

We give agreed notice, settle shared bills, and document the condition of the space.
